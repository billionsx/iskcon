# знание · `hig-progress-indicators`
Источник: https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/progress-indicators
Домены мандата: контролы, анимация
Нормативных положений: 9 (детерминированная выжимка, не пересказ)


## без раздела
- When possible, use a determinate progress indicator.
- People generally prefer a determinate progress indicator, because it helps them gauge what’s happening and how long it will take.
- Don’t switch from the circular style to the bar style.
- Avoid vague terms like loading or authenticating because they seldom add value.
- Don’t make people responsible for initiating every update.
- If you do include a title, don’t use it to explain how to perform a refresh.
- Both versions use an animated image to indicate that the app is performing a task.
- Prefer an activity indicator (spinner) to communicate the status of a background operation or when space is constrained.
- Avoid labeling a spinning progress indicator.
