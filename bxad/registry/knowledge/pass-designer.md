# знание · `pass-designer`
Источник: https://developer.apple.com/pass-designer/
Домены мандата: Wallet, инструменты
Нормативных положений: 5 (детерминированная выжимка, не пересказ)


## без раздела
- You can use your preferred design tool to create images for passes — such as logos, backgrounds, or strip images — then bring them into Pass Designer to complete a pass design.
- Validation Pass Designer validates your pass as you work and alerts you to any issues such as missing required key values and unexpected definitions.
- It can also automatically generate a backward-compatible pass structure from your semantic data that doesn’t require semantic tags, ensuring your pass works where they may not be supported.
- Download the beta Pass Designer beta requires macOS 27 or later.
