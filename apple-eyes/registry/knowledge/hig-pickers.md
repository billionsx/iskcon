# знание · `hig-pickers`
Источник: https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/pickers
Домены мандата: контролы, Calendar
Нормативных положений: 4 (детерминированная выжимка, не пересказ)


## без раздела
- Use predictable and logically ordered values.
- Avoid switching views to show a picker.
- Countdown timer — Displays hours and minutes, up to a maximum of 23 hours and 59 minutes.
- Use a compact date picker when space is constrained.
